About Dr. Salma Khaled

Leading expert in biochemistry and cellular biology research

Biochemical Research & Medical Innovation

Dr. Salma Khaled is a distinguished biology teacher specializing in American diploma programs. With extensive expertise in cellular biology, metabolic processes, and life sciences education, she provides comprehensive instruction in fundamental biological processes including cell division, cellular respiration, photosynthesis, and evolutionary biology for EST and ACT students.
